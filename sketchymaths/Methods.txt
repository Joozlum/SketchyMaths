Operators
'+': addition
'-': subtraction
'*': multiplication
'/': division
'//': floored division
'%': remainder of x/y
'**': raise to power (exponent)

Boolean Operators
'or'
'and'
'not'

Comparisons
'<': less than
'<=': less than or equal
'>': greater than
'>=': greater than or equal
'==': equal
'!=': not equal
'is': object identity
'is not': negated object identity

Bitwise Operators for integers
'|': bitwise or
'^': bitwise exclusive or
'&': bitwise and
'<<': shift left by n bits
'>>': shift right by n bits
'~': invert bits

Math Functions
'acos': acos
'asin': asin
'atan': atan
'atan2': atan2
'ceil': ceil
'cos': cos
'cosh': cosh
'degrees': degrees
'e': e
'exp': exp
'fabs': fabs
'floor': floor
'fmod': fmod
'frexp': frexp
'hypot': hypot
'ldexp': ldexp
'log': log
'log10': log10
'modf':modf
'pi': pi
'pow': pow
'radians': radians
'sin': sin
'sinh': sinh
'sqrt': sqrt
'tan': tan
'tanh': tanh
'percent': divide by 100
'sum': adds together all values in a list e.g. sum([1, 1])
